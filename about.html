Who am I?

Creative Maker & Robotics Designer

I'm a creative maker and designer who's been passionate about robotics for my entire life. As captain of my local FRC team, I've learned that the best engineering happens at the intersection of creativity and passion, It is this combination that fosters more than just technical skill, but a true love for innovation and teamwork.

I like projects where the answer isn’t obvious and the best solution comes from testing, breaking, and refining.

What Else?

Outside of robotics, I run a large Halloween haunted house each year. That means animatronics, lighting effects, set building, sound design, and a lot of late nights making sure everything works exactly how it’s supposed to. I love finding creative uses for other peoples "junk" and turning it into a cohesive immersive experience.

I’m also into FPV drones, PC building, and design work in both 2D and 3D. I enjoy the design process as much as the final result: sketching, modeling, prototyping, and optimizing until it finally works.

My Interests

  • FRC Robotics: I love all aspects of design, build, and competition. There's nothing quite like seeing months of work come together on the field.
  • Haunted House Design: Creating immersive experiences that blend horror, storytelling, and creativity. Currently running a seasonal haunted attraction.
  • 3D Printing & Fabrication: I have been 3d printing since I was six years old and it was the first true passion that sparked my interest in technology. I Previously ran a 3D printing company.
  • Theater Design: I like designing sets, lights, and sound for school theater productions and events.
